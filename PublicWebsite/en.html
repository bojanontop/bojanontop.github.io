O nas

GEOID, Geodetic Engineering Ltd., is a leading surveying company located at 12 Dimičeva Street, in the heart of Ljubljana, in Bežigrad. The company has a rich history and proudly holds a permit for conducting surveying services granted by the Ministry of Environment and Spatial Planning of the Republic of Slovenia, Surveying Administration.

Due to high professional standards, GEOID is a proud member of the Chamber of Engineers of Slovenia, where it is listed in the registry of surveying companies under registration number 0092. This confirms the commitment to high ethical standards, professionalism, and continuous education.

The primary purpose of GEOID is to provide comprehensive surveying solutions tailored to the needs of clients. With a team of highly skilled professionals, they cover a broad spectrum of surveying and engineering services, including cadaster, land surveying, spatial planning, and other related fields.

Their location in Ljubljana enables efficient and prompt response to the needs of clients in the city and surrounding areas. GEOID is known for precision, reliability, and dedication to achieving optimal results for its clients.
